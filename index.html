About Us

Treasuries are the primary units of the State Government’s fiscal system and the points at which public accounts begin. Treasuries conduct transactions of Government money, both receipts and payments and render proper accounts of these transactions to the Accountant General. The Department of Treasuries is under the administrative control of the Finance Department.

  • Integrated Electronic Platform to all stake holders.
  • Introduce effective budget monitoring and ways and means to control through the system
  • Transparency in management of Public Finances.

The Departments through their Chief Controlling Officer (CCO), Controlling Officer (CO), and Drawing and Disbursing Officer (DDO) will execute their financial transactions such as Bill Preparation, Bill Submission to Treasuries, Budget Release and Control, Remittances, Deposits, and Reconciliation of Receipts and Payments etc., in Khajane-II.

Services

Khajane -2 is a resource portal for the use of officials of Government of Karnataka. It is a “Single Stop” for getting all the resources required by Chief Controlling Officers, Drawing and Disbursing Officers, Secretariat Officials, to work smoothly on the Khajane 2, the Integrated Financial Management System of Karnataka.
